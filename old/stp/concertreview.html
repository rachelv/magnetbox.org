I had the opportunity to go to the STP concert in New Haven on December 1st and it was amazing! Two hours (twenty-four songs) of pure listening enjoyment. They played songs from all three albums: Core, Purple, and Tiny Music.

These were the songs they played:

-Pop's Love Suicide  -Dancing Days       -Interstate Love Song
-Crackerman          -Creep              -Lounge Fly
-Meatplow            -Pretty Penny       -Lady Picture Show
-Tumble In The Rough -Seven Caged Tigers -Unglued
-Vasoline            -Trippin' On A Hole -ENCORE-
-Still Remains         In A Paper Heart  -Big Bang Baby
-Silvergun Superman  -Plush              -Dead And Bloated
-Wicked Garden       -Piece Of Pie       -Sex Type Thing
-Daisy               -Big Empty

While Dean and Rob played "Daisy", a circular stage was lowered from the ceiling containing instruments for an acoustic set, which contained "Dancing Days", "Creep", "Pretty Penny", and "Seven Caged Tigers". For songs that used a distotred bacground voice, like "Crackerman", Scott sang into a megaphone. Rob sang backup vocals in "Creep." It was great.

The visual effects were also great. Behind the band was a huge domed screen that showed slides and video clips. Sometimes there were disco balls going and at others there were colored spotlights. It seemed like there was always colored smoke. At the end of the show, during "Big Bang Baby", hundreds of balloons dropped from the ceiling.

The entire show was terrific!
It gets five stars on the Rachel meter.
